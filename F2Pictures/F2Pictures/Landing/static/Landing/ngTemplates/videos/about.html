How we work

Video Production begins with a chat with our clients. Our clients come to us to produce videos for various needs - from training the internal teams, to defining their voice in the marketplace. Over coffee, we discuss these goals and the briefs with our clients. Our creative team then brainstorms strategies to achieve these goals. Our production team then begins the legwork to begin preperation for the actual video shoot.

Brainstorming

In our brainstorming phase, the creative team takes your inputs and works on various creative ideas to deliver the vision. We return to you with ideas for your approval at the end of this phase

Storyboarding and PreViz

Storyboards and PreVizs are visual representations of what the video would look like. We use industry standard storyboarding and PreViz tools to create a framework to produce the video upon.

Production

This is the exciting phase - the actual shoot. We'll handle the locations, talent, permits and the actual shoot. We shoot with best of class camera and audio gear to ensure that the videos we create are not compromised in any way.

Editing

The Edit Suite is where the video comes to life. The final film takes shape here. Our crack team of editors have worked in the Australian Film Industry and have over 20 years of combined editing experience behind them.

Color Correction

Color Correction is the video equivalent of photoshop. This is where we make the colors pop and we create a visual identity for the video.

Feedback and Delivery

At this stage, we upload the videos to Dropbox for client feedback. Once the client signs-off, we master the video and send a hard disk to the client with the final video copied to it.
